BIRTH.

Birth is the act or process of bearing or bringing forth offspring,also referred to in technical contexts as parturition.
In mammals, the process is initiated by hormones which cause the muscular walls of the uterus to contract,
expelling the fetus at a developmental stage when it is ready to feed and breathe.

Growth.

Growth is an increase in physical size, like the growth of a sapling into a mature tree.
There are other kinds of growth, like your sister's personal growth since she started to meditate every day.

Death

Death is the irreversible cessation of all biological functions that sustain an organism.
For organisms with a brain, death can also be defined as the irreversible cessation of functioning of the whole brain,
including brainstem and brain death.
